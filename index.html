More on My Favorite Hobbies


Reading

My favorite genre is historical fiction, especially of the 19th century, set in England or the American Prairie. My favorite authors of historical fiction are Charles Dickens and Jane Austen. I also love mysteries - from Sherlock Holmes and Agatha Christie to Nancy Drew.



DIY Crafting

I love sewing, knitting, crocheting, jewelry-making, weaving, origami, and so much more. I mostly use these skills to make practical things for myself and others, but I also love making gifts and costumes. I also love learning new skills!


Gardening

I've recently started gardening, and I'm having fun growing many plants. I'm growing flowers- which include daffodils, tulips, sunflowers, and tiger lilies, herbs, like basil and lavender, even vegetables like tomatoes!
